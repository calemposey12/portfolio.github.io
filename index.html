While taking classes for Information Systems, I first became exposed to minor programming with languages such as: SQL and Java. In order for me to gain a better understanding about languages, I took the initiative to teach myself Java along with the likes of HTML and CSS in my spare time within the past year.

Being an individual that is willing to constantly learn, self-motivated, and likes solving problems is what makes me different. By not only having techinical skills, but soft skills with is what I bring to the table. Having interaction with others is a key aspect and with a history of customer service with pervious jobs, it plays a vital part with any industry.
